About our Blog Website

This is what we do !

Welcome to our blog website! We are thrilled to present this webpage as part of our university project for the subject "Introduction to Web Programming." Throughout the course, we have honed our skills in various technologies, including GitHub for version control, HTML and CSS for web design, MySQL for database management, and PHP for server-side scripting.

This collaborative project has been a joint effort by Benjamin Peljto, Dzelila Tinjak, and Tarik Panjeta. Together, we have poured our passion and dedication into creating a platform where we can share our knowledge and experiences with you.

Through this blog, we aim to explore various topics related to web programming, sharing valuable insights, tutorials, and best practices with our readers. Our goal is to provide a valuable resource for both beginners and experienced individuals interested in web development.

Thank you for visiting our blog, and we hope you find our content informative, engaging, and inspiring as you embark on your web programming journey.
